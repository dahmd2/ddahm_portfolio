About

I'm currently pursuing a BBA in Business Analytics at the University of Iowa's Tippie College of Business. My studies focus on data analysis, statistical modeling, and business intelligence—giving me the tools to solve real business problems using data.

I recently interned at Uline as an Executive Operations Intern, where I analyzed sales trends, competitor data, and the performance of paid search ad campaigns. My work helped inform strategic marketing decisions.

I'm skilled in SQL, Power BI, and Excel, and I've used these tools to build reports and dashboards that tell stories with data. Whether it’s through visualization or automation, I enjoy turning raw numbers into clear insights.

Outside of analytics, I’m passionate about tennis and was honored as a GMC Girls' Tennis Doubles Athlete of the Year. Competing at the state level taught me discipline, teamwork, and the value of consistency—qualities I bring to every project.
