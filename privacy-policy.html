Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when you visit https://delaniedahm.com (“the Site”).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly from users.

However, the Site uses Google Analytics, a third-party service that collects anonymized data such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Browser and device type
  • Referring websites or search terms
  • General geographic location (based on IP address, which is anonymized)

This data is used solely to improve website performance and user experience. We do not use this data for marketing, advertising, or resale.

2. Use of Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to function. These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the site. You can read more about this in our Cookie Policy.

You can control or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings.

3. Sharing of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data. All data collected through Google Analytics remains with Google and is governed by their privacy policy.

For more information, see Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Your Rights

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact me through the website.
